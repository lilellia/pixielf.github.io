I 2 What kind of syntheses does Jess do alone...?

As Vayne walks through the workshop hallway, he hears an explosion coming from the workshop. There, he finds Philo, covered in soot from her failed synthesis. After a short conversation (where the Mana of Wind tells Vayne that Philo is just used to explosions), they gather new equipment from Someone's Workshop in the Workshop Hallway and dispose of the trash in the incinerator on the Campus Grounds.

I messed up another alchemy synthesis. I thought it would work, too. Vayne helped me clean up this time, but I need to be more careful next time.
II 3 ...has Jess blown anything else up?

That sound... Could it be?

Once again, Vayne witnesses the result of another of Philo's failed syntheses. After he suggests simply telling a teacher, rather than trying to cover it up, the Mana of Light convinces Philo to reveal that she experimented with alchemy before coming to Al-Revis, which is illegal. Instead of going to prison, she was invited to the academy, where she'd be put in special classes. But If she gets kicked out, Jess is gonna go right back to prison, but she can't help doing the syntheses anyway.

Vayne agrees to help her clean up. Fortunately, the equipment isn't broken this time, but it is a mess. It take 5 [Polish Powder] to clean it, but once it's done, the two take the garbage to the incinerator on the Campus Grounds, where they're stopped by the Vice Principal, who—after messing with them a little bit—lets them slide.

In the end, Vayne tells Philo that he'll help her in the future so as to prevent further explosions.

...I blew up the atelier again. It's so weird though...

Vayne helped me clean up again, but I know this is bad. I played around with alchemy before I came to this school, so if I get kicked out, I'll be stuck in jail.

Oh, but next time I do a difficult synthesis, Vayne said he'll help me. Then I can't mess up, right?
III 4 Oh yeah, I promised to help Jess make something.

As promises, Vayne helps Philo with one of her syntheses, and it works perfectly. However, she soon faints unexpectedly, and Vayne rushes her to the Infirmary. When she comes to, she reveals that this isn't super uncommon and that medicine doesn't help.

She tells Vayne that she hasn't completed her homework, which requires him to bring 2 [Nectar] to her. When he returns, she is out of bed and, in response to his concern, explains that she had a really serious illness when she was little and, accordingly, has a shorter lifespan than normal. But her generally carefree attitude about it leaves Vayne confused and wondering if it might even be a joke.

I fainted during synthesis. I felt perfectly fine lately, so it caught me off guard. Vayne saw it, too. I asked him to keep it a secret, but...

Oh well. It can't be helped. I was sick when I was young, and because of it...

Cutscene: Jessica and Mortality (Classroom)
IV 7 I think there's an outdoor class soon...

Vayne and Philo attend a class run by Professor Zeppel in Dragon's Grave: Bone Grove. After gathering items at five gathering spots, the two get into an argument regarding Philo's perspective on death, though Vayne struggles to articulate why he thinks her perspective is unhealthy.

Philo runs off in anger, complaining to the Mana of Wind (who agrees that her argument isn't entirely sound), but she eventually faints, alone. When the class gathers back in the clearing, Vayne notices that Philo didn't return and runs off to find her. (She can be found in the northwest of Bone Grove, near the entrance to Heaven's Road.)

Back at the Academy, Philo lies in the Infirmary, where the other workshop members wait anxiously until she wakes up. Once she does, relieved, they take their leave, but Philo calls for Vayne to wait (inducing a joke from Nikki), and the two apologize and make up.
